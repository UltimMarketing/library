501Connect Attendance

501Connect is greater Gwinnett’s networking event for the nonprofit community. In addition to forming connections within the industry, each event also aligns attendees with external sectors to foster synergy.

Activate Assimilation

Engagement with the GNC provides a plethora of resources. To assist you in accessing all that your membership offers, a complimentary onboarding event, Activate, is provided. And, you’ll be in good company, as the event also provides the opportunity to connect with other new members.

Advocacy & Alignment

Nonprofits play a crucial role in our community, by providing critical services that fill gaps and contribute to economic mobility. With a position of influence, GNC is poised to champion your cause and amplify your impact.

Affiliations Access

Through your affiliation with GNC, additional avenues are unlocked. Access to course work and knowledge centers are provided to the Council of Volunteer Administrators, Georgia Association for Volunteer Administration, National Council of Nonprofits and SCORE.

Board Builder

Serving as a steward of sustainably, boards play a crucial role in nonprofit organizations. Board Builder is an online platform for nonprofits to post available openings for their board of directors. Alignment is promoted through the Gwinnett Coalition’s expansive network.

Candid Community

Formerly known as the Foundation Center and GuideStar, Candid Community combines both resources. As the GNC is a local partner, you can access the platform in person at our office.

Career Connect

Recruiting talent is a top need for nonprofits. Career Connect is an online platform that features open positions in the nonprofit industry. Opportunities are promoted through the Gwinnett Coalition’s expansive network.

Cause Coordinate

By handling specific projects, volunteers can help expand the capacity of the nonprofit. Cause Coordinate is an online platform where nonprofits can promote their project-based volunteer opportunities. Opportunities are promoted through the Gwinnett Coalition’s expansive network.

(c)Social

Where the nonprofit C-Suite convene, (c)Social provides a private networking opportunity for executive level NPOs. A curated gathering, the guest list provides an intimate opportunity to connect with elevated attendees from external sectors.

Elected Officials List

Elected officials play a key role in enacting policies that impact nonprofits in a variety of ways. Building relationships with lawmakers can have a significant impact on a nonprofit’s ability to fulfill its mission. GNC provides elected officials contract information, so you can advocate for your cause.

Exec Connect

Our Circles Roundtables provide nonprofit leaders with the platform to convene in confidence. Through a network of like-minded peers, the program encourages open dialogue, leading to collaborative learning and creative solutions. Exec Connect is comprised of CEOs & Executive Directors (size specific) charged with leading their organizations.

Facility Usage

A space to convene and collaborate, GNC is excited to offer its recently renovated office as a secondary meeting space for members. In addition to two private offices, we also have two conference rooms available for your use with an appointment.

Founders Forum

Scaling a nonprofit is heart work. And mindset matters as much as skillset. At this inspirational event, you will hear from actual nonprofit founders. The panel discussion will include those who started with a passion and have obtained success in the space.

Funding Partnerships

Funding partnerships leverage the contributions of multiple nonprofits to make progress towards a shared goal. Through collaboration, our nonprofit community can be more competitive in the federal funding space. GNC’s grant writer sources and facilitates these funding opportunities.

Grant Calendar

We’ve all heard the saying, time is money. As a nonprofit, researching resources can be quite cumbersome. Based on member priorities, our curated grant calendar cuts down on the preliminary sourcing, so you can focus on preparing your proposal.

Grant Ready Assessment

With a subject-matter expert on staff, the Grant Specialist will meet with members to assess how grant ready the organization is. The nonprofit will be evaluated based on organizational, operational, financial, and programmatic preparedness. A rating will measure the nonprofits’ potential success in securing grants, in addition to connection to resources for further refinement.

Grant Writer Review

The most seasoned nonprofits report a benchmark of 1 in 10 grants that receive awards. Proper positioning when submitting a proposal is paramount to success. Based on your organization’s grant ready assessment score, GNC’s grant specialist will review your grants and make recommendations.

Industry Insights

Through its affiliation with the National Council of Nonprofits (NCN), GNC members are tapped to provide data. Information produced identifies emerging trends, shares proven practices, and promotes solutions. Our relationship with NCN is circular and is a feedback loop that funnels industry information back to you.

L&D Series

Knowledge is power. Through GNC’s industry specific educational events, members can sharpen their skills in pertinent areas such as: board & volunteers, finance & funding, human resources, marketing & communications, operations & management, and more.

Legislative Advisory Council

In its initial year, the Legislative Advisory Council will inform the GNC of nonprofit specific priorities it should consider. Additionally, special speakers will consist of nonprofit minded policy makers. Future facing, feedback will influence the structure of GNC’s advocacy efforts.

Lobbying Lunch & Learn

Lobbying by nonprofits is legal and encouraged by Congress. It is a powerful tool that can foster civic participation that results in stronger public policy. Learn how to get involved in the policymaking process at our lobbying events.

Masterclass Series

At this advanced event, the GNC will team up with professionals in the nonprofit support space. This extended workshop is an expanded deep dive of the L&D Series and will provide knowledge on subject matters important to nonprofits.

Member Events Calendar

Events provide a platform to produce revenue that supports your mission. The GNC website provides a space to post your programs that enhances their exposure. Whether it is an annual gala, fundraising drive or 5K race, you have a place to promote for increased impact.

Member Promotion Kit

Membership in an association builds credibility. It demonstrates your commitment to the industry. To showcase your support, GNC provides a promotion kit that includes ready-to-use copy and graphics.

Nonprofit Directory

Enhanced

Gwinnett’s first nonprofit directory is a comprehensive database that provides a platform for connectivity. Inside the sector, this easily searchable guide reduces service duplication. Outside the sector, this is a tool that other industries can use when sourcing nonprofits to support.

Nonprofit Newsletter

A monthly communication, the Nonprofit Newsletter is packed with information and insights relevant to running your nonprofit.

Prep Your Proposal Program

The Prep Your Proposal (PYP) Program provides a guided overview of top grants. Held digitally, for playback purposes, pertinent information is presented, in addition to an opportunity for Q&A.

Public Policy Symposium

At this action-packed event, the Gwinnett Coalition will provide a forum for nonprofits to connect with local, state, and federal elected officials. In addition, it will curate a conversation on trends and policy issues impacting the industry with the National Council of Nonprofits.

Rise Roundtable

Our Circles Roundtables provide nonprofit leaders with the platform to convene in confidence. Through a network of like-minded peers, the program encourages open dialogue, leading to collaborative learning and creative solutions. Rise Roundtable is for young professionals and is open to emerging leaders at any stage within their organization.

Speakers Bureau

Civic and corporate partners alike are interested in leveraging partnerships to impact the community. Inclusion in our Speakers’ Bureau identifies leading nonprofits who are doing good in greater Gwinnett.
