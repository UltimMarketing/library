What is workers’ compensation?

Workers’ compensation is a form of insurance that provides medical benefits and wage replacement to employees injured in the course of employment. It is designed to help workers recover from injuries and return to work while protecting employers from lawsuits.

Who is eligible for workers’ compensation?

Most employees in the United States are covered by workers’ compensation insurance. This includes full-time, part-time, seasonal, and, in some cases, even temporary workers. Independent contractors and volunteers may not be eligible, as coverage varies by state laws and company policies.
